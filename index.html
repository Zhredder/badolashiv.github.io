About 1.2

David is an agency-represented model, actor & presenter of Scottish & Malaysian-Chinese heritage. He is actually a qualified doctor but works part-time in order to develop his creative side. With over 5 years of experience in both commercial & fashion shoots & professional training in singing & hip hop dancing, David has a broad skill base. He has also worked as a presenter, has experience with green screen & teleprompter/autocue. Combined with his positive energy & on-camera charm, his experience helps him obtain the best result for clients.
